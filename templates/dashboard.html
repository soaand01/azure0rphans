Azure Well-Architected Framework Insights

This dashboard analyzes your Azure App Service Plans based on the following cost optimization principles:

  • Right-sizing: Identify oversized plans with excessive instances
  • Consolidation: Find opportunities to combine underutilized plans
  • Reserved Instances: Evaluate candidates for 1-3 year reservations (up to 55% savings)
  • App Density: Maximize resource utilization by optimizing app-to-instance ratios
  • Tier Optimization: Ensure you're using the most cost-effective tier for your workload

Learn more: Azure Well-Architected Cost Optimization
